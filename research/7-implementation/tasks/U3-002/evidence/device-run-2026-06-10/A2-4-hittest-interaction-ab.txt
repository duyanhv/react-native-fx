READING:
- The deepest hit-test winner is UIVisualEffectView in BOTH states (the UIKit rung installs
  no separate hittable interaction VIEW, unlike the SwiftUI rung's UIPlatformGlassInteractionView).
- The backdrop-independent differentiator is the effect view's UIInteraction set + isInteractive:
    OFF: UIGlassEffect isInteractive=false, interactions=[]
    ON : UIGlassEffect isInteractive=true,  interactions=[_UIFlexInteraction,_UIUpdateLinkViewInteraction]
  _UIFlexInteraction is the system interactive-glass press/flex interaction; it is installed
  ONLY when interactive and absent when static. This is the press machinery, proven without
  any frame-diff over the animated backdrop.
