## Verdict

**Approved.** A clean, well-scoped ratification. The decision — `symbol` is iOS-only in V1 via
`.symbolEffect`; Android AVD/Lottie stay planned/deferred and non-selectable — is recorded consistently
across all three owning docs **and enforced in code**, which is stronger than a doc-only ratify.

## Verified

- **Cardinal rule — true in owning docs:** `24-symbols.md` pins iOS-only V1; `structure.android.md`
  §symbol marks AVD/Lottie `status:planned` / deferred; `data-layer.md` (symbol node, ~240–261) carries
  the iOS `.symbolEffect` rung `supported` and both Android rungs `status: 'planned'`. FX-009 is
  `resolved` with a reconciliation row.
- **Enforced, not just documented:** `select.ts:27` skips `status === 'planned'` rungs (SPINE-013), so
  the Android symbol rungs are non-selectable at runtime and Android degrades to `{via:'none'}` — the
  decision is executable, not aspirational.
- **Scope guard honored:** no iOS/Android symbol *implementation*, no Lottie dependency, no asset
  contract; SPINE-007 (`via:'lib'` naming) left open and owned by DOC-002.
- **Rules gate:** one agnostic `symbol` node, platform-native lowering (#2); curated effect, not
  app-authored (#5); Android honestly absent rather than a faked cross-platform symbol (the law).

## Watch-items (downstream, not defects)
