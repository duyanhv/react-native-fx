- **First-mount translate (Part 3):** both platforms slide on a fresh mount (the `a3d833f`

- **Log-key fix (Part 4)** and **parity (Part 5):** PASS; the platform divergence (top

## Caveats accepted (disclosed in the evidence)

1. **The Android damping finding is source-grounded, not visually isolated** — overshoot at
   ~120 ms sits at/below burst resolution. Acceptable as a catalog *recommendation*; the
   visual confirm is exactly why U7-003 exists.
2. **The strict Fast-Refresh mid-exit instant could not be isolated** — Metro HMR latency
   (~0.7–1.0 s) exceeds the iOS exit (~0.75 s), so the captured runs reloaded just after
   completion. Every guard *outcome* held (no leak, no crash, clean remount) across two
   attempts. The stranded guard's exact trigger ordering remains source-grounded (it was
   already flagged as such in the U7-001 preflight); no further action — dev-only path.
3. **No live iOS banner side-by-side** — notification authorization was ungrantable under
   the code freeze; iOS parity is judged against the canonical documented behavior plus the
   on-device fx captures. Low risk: the iOS values are the platform's own defaults by
   construction (kept-default is the law's preferred outcome).
